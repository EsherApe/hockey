How we handle the information we gather when you visit our web site
We collect no personal information about you when you visit our site unless you choose to provide this information to us. However, we do automatically collect and store the name of the domain from which you access the Internet (.com, .edu, etc.); the date and time you access our site; and the Internet address of the website (such as a search engine or referring page) from which you reached our site. We use the information we collect to count the number of visitors to the different pages on our site, and to help us make our site more useful to visitors like you.

If You Send Us E-mail
You may choose to provide us with personal information, as in e-mail with a comment or question. We use the information to improve our service to you or to respond to your request. Sometimes we forward your e-mail to other persons within the IISHF or within one of our member NGBs who may be better able to help you. Except for authorized law enforcement investigations, we do not share our e-mail with any other outside organizations.

Links to External Websites
Our website has many links to websites of our member NGBs and to websites of teams playing Inline Skater Hockey in both Switzerland and other nations. When you follow a link to another site, you are no longer on our site and are subject to the privacy policy of the new site. Every effort is made to provide accurate and complete information. However, we cannot guarantee that there will be no errors. Security
For site security purposes and to ensure that this service remains available to all users, the IISHF uses software programs to monitor network traffic to identify unauthorized attempts to upload or change information or otherwise cause damage. Unauthorized attempts to upload information or change information on this service are strictly prohibited and may be punishable. Information also may be used for authorized law enforcement investigations. Except for the above purposes, no other attempts are made to identify individual users or their usage habits. Unauthorized attempts to upload information and/or change information on these web sites are strictly prohibited and are subject to prosecution.
